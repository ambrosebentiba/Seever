Our activity

Development of health blockchains

Gramtech develops secure blockchains for rapid sharing of medical information. We have launched a research and development program in partnership with the University of Grenoble Alpes, the Innovacs research federation, and for which we are supported by SATT Linksium (Société d'Accélération de Transfert de Technologie).

Support for health projects

Gramtech accompanies blockchain projects in the health sector on the one hand on the reflection around the use cases and legal constraints specific to the health sector and on the other hand on the implementation of technological solutions.

The challenges of secure health information sharing

Care while protecting patient data

The rapid sharing of a patient's health information between healthcare professionals is crucial today, enabling the most appropriate care to be provided to the patient. However, this information is precious and must remain confidential. The exchange of information must be done with a strict respect of the shared medical secret and the application of the RGPD rules. Gramtech brings solutions to these challenges with a blockchain system adapted to the health sector allowing a fast and secure sharing of information.
